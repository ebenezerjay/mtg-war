MTG WAR!

Just like the card game WAR! Only we are using Magic The Gathering creature cards. Whosever creature has a greater or equal power than the other players creatures toughness, wins the fight and the winning player gets both cards. You win by collecting all the cards in your deck.
Power on the leftToughness on the right

When both creatures have enough power to kill the other creature, or neither have enough to kill eachother WAR! takes place. And those cards get set aside, and each player reveals a new creature. And you keep revealing till a creature wins.
